About Us

Impact Smart CBO is a youth-led community-based organization focused on tackling climate change, waste management, and environmental degradation.

Vision

A resilient, sustainable world where communities thrive in harmony with nature.

Mission

Empower communities through climate-smart practices, innovation, and advocacy for a sustainable future.

Our Services

  • Climate-Smart Agriculture: Sustainable farming practices for productivity and resilience.
  • Environmental Conservation: Community-driven ecosystem restoration and biodiversity protection.
  • Waste Management: Promoting recycling and responsible disposal.
  • Community Empowerment: Training and advocacy for local leadership.
